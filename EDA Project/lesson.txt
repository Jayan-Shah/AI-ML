EDA - Exploratory Data Analysis 

What is EDA?
- Involves summarizing dataset in order to find patters

Steps:-
- Data Cleaning
- Data Trannsformation
- Aggregation and Filtering
- Identifying Patterns, Trends and Correlations

Key Patters we are looking for:
- Relationship between variables
- Distribution of variables(histogram,boxplots)
- Outlies or anomalies

- Summary statistics. Visual Insights and Hypothesis Generation...
